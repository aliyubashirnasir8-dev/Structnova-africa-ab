My Journey

Growing up in Nigeria, I witnessed firsthand the critical need for robust infrastructure and the importance of structural safety in our communities. This experience ignited my passion for civil engineering and my commitment to contributing meaningfully to the development of safe, sustainable structures across Africa.

As I progress in my engineering education, I am equally invested in developing software solutions that can make structural engineering more accessible and efficient. My vision is to create tools that empower engineers to perform complex calculations with ease, ultimately leading to safer and more reliable structures.

Career Goals

  • Contribute to major infrastructure projects in Nigeria
